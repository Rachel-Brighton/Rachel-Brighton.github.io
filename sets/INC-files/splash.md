This set is comprised of preconstructed decks for use in the Touhou Block Constructed format. Each deck focuses fully on the plot of a single game, introducing its cast and telling the story of the game's events. All cards are legal in all block constructed play, and may be mixed and matched freely with the rest of the format. You can check out each deck by clicking on its name here:
